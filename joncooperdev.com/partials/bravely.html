About The Project

Bravely Default is a RPG developed by Square Enix and a child of the Final Fantasy series. I was tasked with developing a responsive, multilingual site for the game.

Mobile Views

In additional to the desktop view, I was responsible for delivering a responsive experience to provide a seamless experience accross multiple devices.

Behind the Curtain

My responsibility was to complete all of the front-end developement for the site, and to work with Nintendo to hand off the code for integration on their site.
