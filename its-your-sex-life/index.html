IT'S YOUR SEX LIFE

It's Your Sex Life is MTV's Emmy and Peabody Award-winning public information campaign to support young people in making responsible decisions about their sexual health. IYSL required a combination of HTML, CSS3/SCSS, JavaScript, and AngularJS built on a Ruby on Rails back-end and connecting to a JSON API.
