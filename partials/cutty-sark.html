About Cutty Sark

Cutty Sark is an international whisky brand founded in 1923. My responsibility was to improve their presence by executing the front-end development for their US website.

Mobile Views

It was important to the client that the site be accessible on a variety of devices. To accommodate, we used CakePHP to deliver a unique experience, based on the user agent.

Behind the Curtain

My specific contributions to the project were to consult with designers on the adaptive views and to code the entirety of the front-end and much of the back-end.
