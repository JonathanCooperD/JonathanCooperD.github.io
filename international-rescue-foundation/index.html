International Rescue Foundation

The interactive Outcomes and Evidence Framework supports humanitarian and development professionals to design effective programs. It delivers key information on outcomes related to health, safety, education, economic wellbeing, and power through theories of change. As the lead engineer on the project I worked closely with the client to first provide a proof of concept prototype and then to architect and deliver a React application that can be run both on and offline.
